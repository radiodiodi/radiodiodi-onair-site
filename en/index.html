Emerging form Otaniemi, Radiodiodi is 100% student-made, commercial but non-profit radio station. Radiodiodi's broadcast in 2017 will be the sixth one, and it takes place between 18.-30.4. Every year dozens of student volunteers do immense amounts of work to get the station running. Broadcasts are hosted 24/7 by hundreds of students around Aalto University.

The whole Helsinki area can listen our program. There lives almost 400 000 people in our calculated coverage area!

Info for corporate partners

Radiodiodi is commercial but non-profit radio station. Cooperation and partnership with all kinds of companies is therefore vital to our operations. Our project can be supported by various ways: money, material support or almost anything radio-related (or not) is happily accepted!

Join us!

Interested in being a part of this? Want to learn to use modern sound technology? Intrigued by participating in the awesome team with neverending fun? Radiodiodi offers easy solution for all these: become a part of us! Dozens of people with every kind of competence are needed, there is always something to do! Nowadays we start the whole project again immediatly when the previous one ends, because we need to keep the team doing things around the year, not just the last two weeks of April. Free-form applications are always accepted, more info below!

Producers takes care of the production of commercials and the actual radio programs. Producers ensure the program hosts have the possibility to focus fully on the program itself, without the connsern of faders and levels etc. We grant a personal producer for every program host, unless they want to produce themselves. Producers are also responsible for the availability of the music that the hosts wants to play in their shows. Producing commercials means figuring out the idea, recording and editing. The Chief of producers is the Editor in Chief.
Studio technicians are responsible for the operation and construction of the studio. Devices and technology are built and refined well in advance prior to the execution of the broadcast. When the containers arrive the only thing to be made is the physical construciton and hooking everything up. In the lead of Studio technicians is the Chief of Studio.
IT-people keep the complicated IT infrastrucutre in control. Construction and maintenance of a proper IT system is vital for Radiodiodi's production capability. Everything must stay up and running continuously without exception. The management of the IT department is in the hands of IT-manager.
Broadcast Technicians are small but vital part of the project. The actual broadcasting is doubtlessly important part of Radiodiodi. Their territory consists of FM and web broadcasts and the construction and maintenance of those both. Chief of Broadcast in the lead.
Marketing and visibility are handled by the Media Team. Useful skills for the operations are lightning-fast skills in GIMP, photography and sound editing. Secretary of the Board manages the Media Team.
Promoters sell radio commercials and negotiate great sponsorship deals. They are in charge of the company relations in a wide scale. This is managed by our CEO.

How to get along?

Inspired? Intrigued? Express your feelings towards the job you are interested in by mailing us a short free-form description of yourself to board@radiodiodi.fi! All other questions are also welcom via that mail or our Telegram channel!

However, please note a few things:

  • In Radiodiodi we use Slack for our internal communications. Using this platform is a prerequisite for participation.
  • Even though big part of the project is pre-made throughout the year, the workload in April will be huge. Freshmen are welcome to participate of course, but keep in mind that we count on your dedication even though the sixth beer-pong -tournament surely would be fun thing to participate ;)
  • Also, if you happen to be interested in hosting some big radio program, morning show for example, be in touch!
  • Didn't find anything particularly interesting above? Have something else in mind? Or just want to be part of our awesome team, be in touch! As always, ther will always be work for fun and motivated people!
