Emerging form Otaniemi, Radiodiodi is 100% student-made, commercial but non-profit radio station. Radiodiodi's broadcast in 2017 will be the sixth one, and it takes place between 18.-30.4. Every year dozens of student volunteers do immense amounts of work to get the station running. Broadcasts are hosted 24/7 by hundreds of students around Aalto University.

This all means that the collaboration between Radiodiodi and companies of all industries is extremely vital to our operations. Without sponsors the most awesome student radio broadcast simply wouldn't be possible to execute.

Media Info

  • Essential part of the 1st of May time of the technology students in Finland!
  • Listeners of FM broadcast are mainly from Otaniemi but other students in Helsinki area listen our broadcast too, as well as already graduated people.
  • Radiodiodi is played non-stop in break rooms and canteens around Otaniemi Campus Area.
  • Record amount of online listeners is 300 unique computers. So far we have no data about the amount of FM-listeners.
  • Radiodiodi is constantly gaining more fans: during the year 2016 our Facebooks' likes alone increased 40%
  • In 2017 we will increase the amount of marketing to non-Aalto students.

Ad bundles

Our sponsors and parters are highly visible and audible in Otaniemi during april. Our studio is in a cargo container in an extremely central location, right next to the Student Union building. In Otaniemi, you can't miss us!

  • Posts made in accordance with the wishes of the partner to our social media channels.
  • Partners logo placed in our website.

The Main Partner (1000€)

  • Same as in partnership, but the commercial is played 30 times a day.
  • Option to book two (2) hours of radio broadcast time before the actual program hosts get to choose. This is an excellent opportunity to tell about recruitment or the general buzz of the company.
  • Chance ot bring your promotional mterials (pens, caps, etc.) to be shared from our containers in Alvari Square.

Maximum price 0.18 per minute.

Our forms of partnership are always negotiable and the payment can be made with money, donation of equipment or some other radio related (or not) way. If you are interested or have ANY questions about our project, do not hesitate to contact corporate@radiodiodi.fi.
